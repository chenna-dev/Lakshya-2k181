Welcome to Yantrarora…,
1.	Yantrarora deals mainly with the Robotics, which deals with the design, construction, operation, and application of robots, as well as computer systems for their control, sensory feedback, and information processing
2.	. In this yantrarora we will conduct a competition between the robots.
In this competition the robots need to follow the track given to them, and at the end of the track we will be having candle placed on the glass of height 10cm which the robot needs to blow it off. The track consists of smooth surfaces, rough surfaces , slopes and  irregular surface .
3.	Inorder to blow off the candle the robots can use any type of method like arms, blower, cloths attached to its arm and many more…,
