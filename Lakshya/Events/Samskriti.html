We are glad to inform you that, LBRCE is organizing 10th National Level Technical and cultural Symposium LAKSHYA-2K15, a one day event, on 19th September 2015. It is open to all B. Tech, M. Tech, MCA and MBA students. During LAKSHYA SAMSKRITI-2K15 various cultural events like 

1. Dance
2. Singing
3. Mimicry
4 .Skits/Drama 

are being organized. In addition to the above on  Spot cultural 
events are also being organized. Attractive prizes are being 
offered for the winners of events in this programme. 

For this first ever winter cultural fest being organised in the 
capital region of Andhra Pradesh, we request you to conduct 
intra college selections and send one best team/participant 
for each contest mentioned above. We expect enthusiastic 
participation from esteemed college like yours for making 
our evening glorious.
