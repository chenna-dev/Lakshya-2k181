Lakshya



Tonnes of knowledge, lots of interactions, loads of fun, heaps of happiness.Who would forsake a day of Euphoria? In the midst of all the hectic academic schedule, days like Lakshya can never be forsaken. LAKSHYA is a national level technical symposium, annually organised by Lakireddy BaliReddy College of Engineering. Lakshya is a perfect conglomeration where expertise, passion, innovation, fun go hand in hand . With events like Srujana (Paper Presentation), Pragna (Poster Presentation), Nipuna (Project Expo), Medha(Technical Quiz), Lakshya can be regarded as an Arena to showcase thousands of talents. Besides these, Art Expo, Central events and jillions of Spot events are blended together along with the Cultural Events that would satisfy the participantís quest for knowledge and drench them with waves of excitement and fun. Anyone with sheer enthusiasm is appreciated to participate and get a handful of insights, exposure that would cater to their development.
