WELCOME TO MECHANICAL DEPARTMENT

About Department

The Department of Mechanical Engineering has been in existence ever since the college had its inception in 1998. The department offers B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering and M.Tech in Thermal Engineering. The B.Tech program had its initiation in the year 1998 with annual intake of 60 students which was raised to 120 in the year 2010, again to180 in the year 2016. The M.Tech program was initiated in the year 2010 with an intake of 18. The B.Tech Program is accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA). The Department has good infrastructure in terms of the state-of-the art laboratories, workshops and computer centres as required for UG and PG programmes. The department has sponsored projects worth of rupees 1.2 Crores to its credit funded by various government organisations.


The Department has well qualified and experienced faculty in thermal, design and production streams. The department has 36 teaching faculty with 9 Doctorates and the rest with M.Tech. Seven faculties are pursuing Ph.D in different Universities. The faculties are engaged in research activities (including funded projects) in their areas of specialisation to contribute the knowledge transfer in their respective fields. Numerous research papers have been published in National, International Journals and Conferences. The Department frequently organizes guest lectures by eminent professors across India and abroad, scientists from various research organisations and experts from industries to bridge the gap between academia and industry. Faculties are encouraged in improving their academic qualifications and also in getting expertise by attending different FDPs, workshops and conferences. The faculties are encouraged to present research papers at various conferences and workshops, and also to participate in various co-curricular and extracurricular activities.
