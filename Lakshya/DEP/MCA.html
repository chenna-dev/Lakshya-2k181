WELCOME TO MCA DEPARTMENT

About Department

Master of Computer Applications (MCA) department is established in 2002 with the fast changing ways of business, there has been a tremendous increase in demand for applications software. Software cannot be manufactured; it has to be produced by creative people. So the work of software development is a human-intensive activity. To meet the needs of the above requirements, there is an immense need to produce high quality application developers.

The department is having well qualified and experienced faculty members. The teaching methodology in the department goes beyond fulfilling the syllabus requirements of the curriculum, to meet the today's industry needs. Faculty motivates and guides the students to do mini projects in core subjects. Special focus is being given to develop Communication and Soft Skills. The department has very good association with industries to uplift the student's practical knowledge.



Srujana-the PAPER PRESENTATION



To exhibit the creative ability in identifying and communicating the views and ideas with regard to technological developments

TOPICS

  • Block chain Technology
  • Software Engineering
  • Wireless and sensor Networks
  • Cloud Computing
  • Datamining and Machine Intelligence
  • Internet of Things(IOT)
  • Big Data Analytics
  • Computer Vision

RULES

The registration fee for Srujana is Rs 250 per team. A maximum of two members are allowed in a team.

Selection of your papers will be intimated through mail or phone by 20th Dec 2018, latest .

It is mandatory to carry college id cards during Lakshya-2k18.

Presentation time is 8 minutes (can vary based on number of papers to be presented in the session). - 6 minutes for presentation - 2 minutes for queries.

The organizers reserve the right to change/update the rules of the contest at any point of time and will do their best to inform to participants of the same. The decision taken by the judges is final.
