WELCOME TO ECE DEPARTMENT

About Department

The department of Electronics and Communication Engineering was established in the year 1998 with an intake of 40 seats in UG program and in 2016 PG program “VLSI & Embedded Systems” was introduced with an intake of 30 seats. Student intake is increased from 40 to 60 in the academic year 2001-02 and 60 to 90 in the academic year 2002-03 and 90 to 120 in the academic year 2008-09 and 120 to 180 in the academic year 2012-13. ECE is the largest department in the college.


The total faculty strength of the ECE department is 45, among them 12 are Professors, 08 are Associate Professors and 25 are Assistant Professors. All are M.Tech.,’s. In the department, there are totally 17 supporting staff, among them one Foreman, Two Programmers, 4 technicians, 4 Lab Assistants, one Jr.Assistant, 3 Helper and 2 attendars.




Srujana-The Paper Presentation



To exhibit the creative ability in identifying and communicating the views and ideas with regard to technological developments

TOPICS

  • VLSI/Embedded Systems
  • MEMS/NEMS/NANO Electronics
  • Signal Processing
  • Anternas and Communications
  • Internet of Things(IOT)

RULES

The registration fee for Srujana is Rs 250 per team. A maximum of two members are allowed in a team.

Selection of paper will be intimated through mail or phone by 20th Dec 2018, latest.

It is mandatory to carry college id cards during Lakshya-2k18.

Presentation time is 8 minutes (can vary based on number of papers to be presented in the session). - 6 minutes for presentation - 2 minutes for queries.

The organizers reserve the right to change/update the rules of the contest at any point of time and will do their best to inform to participants of the same. The decision taken by the judges is final.
