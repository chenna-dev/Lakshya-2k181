WELCOME TO IT DEPARTMENT

About Department

Department of information Technology, started in 1999, with an intake of 40 in Graduation and increased intake of 60 in 2001 over the years. The department grew in stature and today, it is one of the most preferred departments in the state. The department has very good mix of experienced faculty who bring wisdom and young faculty who induce dynamism and enthusiasm.




SRUJANA-the PAPER PRESENTATION



To exhibit the creative ability in identifying and communicating the views and ideas with regard to technological developments

TOPICS

  • Data Mining & Machine learning
  • Crypthography & Network Security
  • Cloud Computing & Ubiquitous Computing
  • Bigdata Analytics
  • Block Chain Technologies & Cryptocurrency
  • Internet Of Things & Smart City
  • Deep Learning & Image Processing

RULES

The registration fee for Srujana is Rs 250 per team. A maximum of two members are allowed in a team.

Selection of papers will be intimated through mail or phone by 20th Dec 2018, latest .

It is mandatory to carry college id cards during Lakshya-2k18.

Presentation time is 8 minutes (can vary based on number of papers to be presented in the session). - 6 minutes for presentation - 2 minutes for queries.

The organizers reserve the right to change/update the rules of the contest at any point of time and will do their best to inform to participants of the same. The decision taken by the judges is final.
